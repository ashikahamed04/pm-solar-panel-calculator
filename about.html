About Us

PM Surya Ghar Solar Calculator is a free online tool created to help Indian households estimate rooftop solar system size and government subsidy under the PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana.

By using your electricity bill or monthly electricity units, this calculator provides an estimated solar system capacity (kW), number of panels required, and the applicable central subsidy amount.

This website is intended for informational and educational purposes only. Actual solar system sizing, generation, and subsidy approval may vary based on location, electricity distribution company (DISCOM), roof conditions, and government rules.

Our goal is to make solar adoption simple, transparent, and easy to understand for everyone.
